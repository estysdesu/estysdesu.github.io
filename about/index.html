EstysDesu Meaning

For about six months during college I took the oppurtunity to internship abroad in Japan. This experience has had a significant impact on my life and who I am today. Estys desu (エステスです) is Japanese for "I am Estys." Whereas, Estys is just a combination of my last and first names. I've adopted the Japanese meaning to symbolize that I am the accumulation of my life experiences.
