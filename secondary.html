Opal Iced Coffee Maker:
The Ultimate Kitchen Appliance

The brainchild of Thomas Dzierzak and Erik Schutzman, the Opal Iced Coffee Maker came to be at the GE Appliances Hack the Home: Mega Hackathon hosted at FirstBuild in Louisville, KY. The new kitchen appliance was built in less than 24 hours from a FirstBuild Opal Nugget Ice Maker and a Mr. Coffee® coffee maker. In addition to successfully turning 200°F water into 55°F hot-brewed iced coffee, the Opal Iced Coffee Maker was recognized by US Steel and came in first for their sponsor challenge.

Tevo Tarantula 3D Printer

What started as a few hundred dollar stock 3D printer kit, turned into a hobby and a love for design, automation, and all things additive manufacturing. Two years after my initial kit purchase, I've modified my printer by adding a second Z-axis motor, replacing all the acrylic mounting pieces with custom aluminum ones, replacing the inbuilt heater on the aluminum bed with a silicone heater pad, and adding wireless printing functionality with Octoprint flashed on a Raspberry Pi Zero.

Drinkin' Bros. Podcast Skill

The Drinkin' Bros. community has over 95K members worldwide. They release multiple podcasts on a variety of topics every week and recently started a few spin off series. In order to help listeners keep up with podcast, I created the Drinkin' Bros. Podcast Skill for Amazon Alexa. It allows the user to ask for basic episode information such as "When was the newest episode?" or "What was Episode 178 about?" Future functionality I'm hoping to add is the ability to ask Alexa to play the audio of the podcast through the skill.

MaxLand Cincy:
Optimizing Cincinnati's Unused Property

MaxLand Cincy was the product of the RevolutionUC Hackathon. My team and I developed a web app and alexa skill that utilized data from Cincinnati's open data portal to highlight the vacant properties as well as most recent purchase value across the city. The app aims to give suggestions on ways to get people in those properties, such as by rezoning a single family home to a multifamily home.
